    Estéfano S. Pietragalla

    Product Designer
    & UX Research

    I have over 7 years of experience in Digital Products, working across the entire UX and Product Design lifecycle, from discovery to delivery. I have worked on high-impact projects at Banco do Brasil and TCU, leading DesignOps and Design Systems initiatives, focusing on research, usability, accessibility, metrics, and data visualization, using methods such as Design Thinking, Service Design, and Lean UX. I also teach in the areas of Design, UX, and Innovation.
